ADOPT A FRIEND

Looking to adopt your new best friend? A full stack application that uses cheerio (node package) to scrape through three dog adoptions agency sites in SF. MongoDB was used for modeling and to allow users to leave comments on his or her favorite doggos!

SIP

Ever been overwhelmed with the amount of choices when choosing a place to grab a drink with your friends? An application using Google Maps and Yelp APIs to help users battle 'paralysis by analysis'.

BARK

A full stack social media application for individuals to share their thoughts or follow their favorite influencers! Passport (node package) was used for authentication and Sequelize for modeling. The weather widget grabs the location of the user and will only display relevant posts depending on the user's location!

2DO

A full stack application to help individuals stay on track! MySQL was used to allow users to create, update, and delete his or her 2DO tasks. MVC framework was used for the purpose of easy maintenance and scalability as well as handlebars for the template engine.
